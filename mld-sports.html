HOW IT
WORKS

01
Pick your team
Choose from all 30 MLB franchises. Your team's colours show up on the field with custom pegs for every runner.
02
Roll two dice
Each combination maps to an outcome — singles, home runs, strikeouts, sac flies. 21 results, zero ambiguity.
03
Play a full 9-inning game
Top and bottom halves, runners on base, tag-ups, double plays — the real game mechanics, no shortcuts.
04
Build in Season Mode
Draft upgrades between games. Swap outcomes in your deck. Beat 5 opponents to win the pennant.

Roguelite Progression

SEASON
MODE

Play five games against escalating CPU opponents. After every game, a scout slides into your dugout with three upgrade offers — swap one dice outcome in your deck for something better.

Win the pennant. Start a new season. Build a different deck. No two runs play the same.

🃏
21-slot deck with a guaranteed minimum of 8 outs — infinite innings are mathematically impossible.
📈
Four rarity tiers: Common, Rare, Epic, and Cursed. Cursed cards give you something great and take something away.
🤖
CPU opponents have their own decks that scale with difficulty — by World Series, they've got five upgrades on you.
💾
Season state saves automatically. Pick up exactly where you left off.
